Who are we?

Harmony Voices is a group of young Adventists(16 to 21 years) from the Nyeri Central SDA Ambassadors. We aim to proclaim the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ through harmonious music.

Mission: To preach Christ to the whole world through our music, lifestyle and devotion and in so doing bring many to the fold as instructed by Christ in Matthew 28: 18 -20.

With the help of our pastors, patrons, parents and seniour youth, we hope to achieve this objective, and pray that you keep us in your prayers as we await the second coming of Jesus Christ. Shalom

Harmony Voices is composed of young people from the age of 16 to 21 years. In the year 2018, we decided to stand as an independent group from the Nyeri Central Youth so as to address issues affecting people within our age bracket.
